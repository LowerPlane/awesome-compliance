## Compliance Frameworks

*Major compliance and security frameworks*

### SOC 2 (Service Organization Control)

- **[AICPA SOC 2](https://www.aicpa.org/soc)** - Trust Services Criteria for security, availability, processing integrity, confidentiality, and privacy.
- **[SOC 2 Academy](https://soc2.academy)** - Educational resources for SOC 2 compliance.
- **[SOC 2 FYI](https://soc2.fyi)** - Guide comparing available solutions for SOC 2.

### ISO Standards

- **[ISO 27001](https://www.iso.org/isoiec-27001-information-security.html)** - Information security management system (ISMS) standard.
- **[ISO 27002](https://www.iso.org/standard/75652.html)** - Code of practice for information security controls.
- **[ISO 27017](https://www.iso.org/standard/43757.html)** - Cloud security controls based on ISO 27002.
- **[ISO 27018](https://www.iso.org/standard/76559.html)** - Protection of personally identifiable information (PII) in public clouds.
- **[ISO 27701](https://www.iso.org/standard/71670.html)** - Privacy information management system (PIMS) extension to ISO 27001.
- **[ISO 22301](https://www.iso.org/iso-22301-business-continuity.html)** - Business continuity management systems.
- **[ISO 9001](https://www.iso.org/iso-9001-quality-management.html)** - Quality management systems.

### Healthcare Compliance

- **[HIPAA](https://www.hhs.gov/hipaa/)** - Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act for healthcare data protection.
- **[HITRUST CSF](https://hitrustalliance.net/hitrust-csf/)** - Healthcare-focused security framework and certification.
- **[HITECH](https://www.hhs.gov/hipaa/for-professionals/special-topics/hitech-act-enforcement-interim-final-rule/index.html)** - Health Information Technology for Economic and Clinical Health Act.
- **[21 CFR Part 11](https://www.fda.gov/regulatory-information/search-fda-guidance-documents/part-11-electronic-records-electronic-signatures-scope-and-application)** - FDA regulations for electronic records and signatures.

### Financial & Payment Security

- **[PCI DSS](https://www.pcisecuritystandards.org)** - Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard for payment processing.
- **[SOX](https://www.soxlaw.com)** - Sarbanes-Oxley Act for financial reporting and corporate governance.
- **[GLBA](https://www.ftc.gov/business-guidance/privacy-security/gramm-leach-bliley-act)** - Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act for financial institutions.
- **[FINRA](https://www.finra.org)** - Financial Industry Regulatory Authority requirements.
- **[SEC Cybersecurity Rules](https://www.sec.gov/cybersecurity)** - Securities and Exchange Commission cybersecurity disclosure requirements.

### Data Privacy Regulations

- **[GDPR](https://gdpr.eu)** - General Data Protection Regulation for EU data privacy.
- **[CCPA](https://oag.ca.gov/privacy/ccpa)** - California Consumer Privacy Act.
- **[CPRA](https://cppa.ca.gov)** - California Privacy Rights Act (CCPA 2.0).
- **[LGPD](https://iapp.org/resources/article/brazilian-data-protection-law-lgpd-english-translation/)** - Brazilian General Data Protection Law (Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados).
- **[PIPEDA](https://www.priv.gc.ca/en/privacy-topics/privacy-laws-in-canada/the-personal-information-protection-and-electronic-documents-act-pipeda/)** - Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (Canada).
- **[PDPA](https://www.pdpc.gov.sg/overview-of-pdpa/the-legislation/personal-data-protection-act)** - Personal Data Protection Act (Singapore).

### Government & Federal

- **[FedRAMP](https://www.fedramp.gov)** - Federal Risk and Authorization Management Program for cloud services.
- **[FISMA](https://www.cisa.gov/topics/cyber-threats-and-advisories/federal-information-security-modernization-act)** - Federal Information Security Modernization Act.
- **[NIST Cybersecurity Framework](https://www.nist.gov/cyberframework)** - Framework for improving critical infrastructure cybersecurity.
- **[NIST 800-53](https://csrc.nist.gov/publications/detail/sp/800-53/rev-5/final)** - Security and privacy controls for federal systems.
- **[CMMC](https://www.acq.osd.mil/cmmc/)** - Cybersecurity Maturity Model Certification for defense contractors.
- **[ITAR](https://www.pmddtc.state.gov/ddtc_public/ddtc_public?id=ddtc_public_portal_itar_landing)** - International Traffic in Arms Regulations.
- **[StateRAMP](https://www.stateramp.org)** - State-level cloud security authorization program.

### Industry-Specific

- **[TISAX](https://www.enx.com/tisax/)** - Trusted Information Security Assessment Exchange for automotive industry.
- **[NERC CIP](https://www.nerc.com/pa/Stand/Pages/CIPStandards.aspx)** - Critical Infrastructure Protection for energy sector.
- **[FERPA](https://www2.ed.gov/policy/gen/guid/fpco/ferpa/index.html)** - Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act for student data.
- **[COPPA](https://www.ftc.gov/legal-library/browse/rules/childrens-online-privacy-protection-rule-coppa)** - Children's Online Privacy Protection Act.
